Our Commitment To You

We want sdg employees to be passionate about what they do and where they work. By creating a solid foundation within our workplace, we can maintain long-lasting relationships with both consultants and clients.

Company Cabins 

We have two company cabins just north of Garrison for employees to share with their friends and family throughout the year - what's more Minnesotan than that?! Whether you're up there for our annual sdg fishing tournament or there for a weekend getaway, you're guaranteed a good time. 

Good Times

To keep our consultants connected with each other, sdg hosts several company events during the year. Whether it's a happy hour with coworkers, a holiday party with spouses, or a baseball game with families, we are always looking for ways to get you involved.

Collaborative Learning

The learning opportunities at sdg are endless. Consultants are encouraged to attend and lead internal presentations and book clubs, speak at local conferences, keep up on the latest technologies, and most importantly, share their knowledge with other sdg'ers.

Perks 

We have several employee perks like attending training conferences, PTO, 401 (k) with a company match, Costco/Sam's Club Memberships, multiple recognition programs and a technology reimbursement to help you keep up with the latest and greatest tech toys. 

Leadership Opportunities 

If blogging is your thing, or health and wellness or maybe presenting to the group, sdg provides that connection for our employees.  These committees are operated and controlled by our employees, helping direct the areas that are most important to them

Benefits

Health, dental, vision benefits are important to our employees and their families.  Sdg offers a variety of options that best fit the needs of our family.  In addition we offer short and long term disability and life insurance at no cost to our employees.  We also have 401(k) and Roth plans for our employees to participate.
