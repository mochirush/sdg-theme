Who We Are

Solution Design Group (sdg) is an IT consulting firm based in Golden Valley, MN. We deliver IT solutions around Microsoft and Open Source technologies and utilize our Project Delivery Team to help round out these service offerings. We have over 100 consultants and just over 20 operations members that make up the sdg Team. Most of our customers can be found locally around the Twin Cities, but we are also involved with a few projects across the country – something we're pretty excited about.

How We Got Here

sdg has been doing business for almost 25 years. Bill Francomb started the company in 1989, and is currently the Chairman of the Board, one of the three principal shareholders, and a billable consultant at sdg – giving him a unique employee-level perspective. Jeff Daniel, long-time friend and previous coworker of Bill, joined sdg just two years later in 1991. With several years of technical experience and a passion for programming, Jeff became the second founder of sdg. He is also a billable consultant, the Director of Project Delivery, and the current CEO at sdg.

Keith Martin, the third and final founder of sdg, joined Bill and Jeff in 2004. Keith came on board to help realign the company's strategy and find the best and brightest people for sdg. Keith became the President of sdg in 2006 and still holds that title today.

What We Live By

Along with solid leadership and great people, a company is defined and built by its Core Values. At sdg, we are committed to ours and have been since the very beginning:

  • Superior Customer Service
  • Exceptional Employee Experience
  • Responsible Corporate Citizenship

We believe the key to our continued success is staying true to these values.
