About Us

Solution Design Group, Inc. (sdg) is an IT consulting and custom application development firm. We deliver IT solutions around Microsoft and Open Source technologies and utilize our Project Delivery Team to help round out these service offerings. We’ve been around since 1989 and are currently located in Golden Valley, MN.

Join the Team

At sdg, we look for people that are the right fit for our culture -- having the right attitude is equally as important to us as having the right skills. We hire talented technologists who will uphold our values, take pride in representing the sdg brand, and care about our customers and our community as much as we do.
